■ BRAND LANDSCAPE
Erbauer — Volume King
276 SKUs, avg GBP11.45. Screwfix's house brand floods the entry market. GBP0-5 band: 98 SKUs. No one competes on price here. But avg rating 4.56 — not cheap junk, genuinely good value. The Screwfix channel IS Erbauer's channel.
Bosch — Broad Portfolio
202 SKUs, avg GBP19.62. Covers every price band evenly. The only brand with real presence across GBP0-5 (74 SKUs) through GBP100+ (4 SKUs). Strategy: be everywhere. But avg rating 4.53 — solid, not exceptional.
DeWalt — Tradesman's Choice
133 SKUs, avg GBP12.57. Focus on masonry (55 SKUs) and mid-range sets. The 100-piece set (GBP26.99, 1,052 reviews) is the #1 reviewed product in the entire category. DeWalt owns "I need a reliable set for work" positioning.
Wera — Premium Screwdriver
108 SKUs, avg GBP39.78. Highest avg price among top 5. Owns the screwdriver bit niche: 43 bit sets + 19 bits + 29 other. The specialist's specialist. If you need screwdriver bits, Wera = "I care about quality enough to pay double."
Trend — Router Monopolist
70 SKUs, avg GBP34.99. 55 out of 70 are router bits. Zero presence under GBP5 — Trend doesn't do cheap. Avg rating 4.66 — highest among brands with 50+ SKUs. They own a niche and defend it with quality.
■ STRUCTURAL INSIGHTS
The GBP0-5 Trap
238 products (23% of total) in the GBP0-5 band. Erbauer has 98, Bosch has 74. Margin is razor-thin. This is a volume game — only works if you're Screwfix (Erbauer) or a global OEM (Bosch). Any new entrant here is margin suicide.
The GBP20-50 Sweet Spot
269 products (Masonries, Router Bits, DeWalt sets). This is where the margin lives. Erbauer has 45 SKUs here, Bosch 42, Trend 42, Wera 41. But look at avg ratings: Bosch 4.81, Milwaukee 4.70, Trend 4.73 — the premium brands dominate quality. Erbauer at 4.40 is the weakest.
Individual Stills Rule
527 individual bits vs 492 sets. But sets avg GBP20-39 vs individual avg GBP16. ABP (avg basket price) is higher for sets. The play: individual bits for tradesmen replacing worn stock, sets for new buyers and gift market.
Material Gap = Opportunity
899 out of 1,019 products don't specify material (HSS, Cobalt, Carbide). Only Diamond (74), Titanium (16), Carbide (15) are called out. If you brand "Cobalt HSS" or "Ground HSS" clearly, you differentiate in a sea of unspecified metal.
■ RECOMMENDATIONS
▶ GBP5-15 工具鋼單支 — 全場只有 Erbauer 和 Titan 在 GBP5-10 有規模（各60、6 SKUs）。高品質 HSS 或 Cobalt 單支可輕鬆切入，定價 GBP7-12。
▶ Hex Shank Masonry 組合包 — DeWalt 有55款 Masonry 但集中在 GBP10-30。Erbauer 有56款但便宜。GBP15-25 的「Masonry + SDS 混合包」是缺口。
▶ 不要碰 GBP0-5 — 238 SKUs 已是紅海。除非你是 Screwfix 自有品牌（Erbauer），否則毛利不夠。
▶ Router Bit 是護城河 — Trend 一家佔55/91款，4.66 rating。不是不能打，但要有差異化（比如 Carbid? tip router bits，Trend 沒打的規格）。
▶ 材質標示是零成本的差異化 — 88% 產品沒標材質。把 HSS / HSS-Co / Carbide / Diamond 寫清楚，在同類產品中立刻突出。

STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS // 戰略建議
P0 Security Bits 打頭陣 — 5 種 Security 頭型同時推出，Screwfix 市場上沒有一個品牌做到。定價 £5-15，遠離 Erbauer 紅海。
P0 材質鋼號全標示 — A-2 / S-2 / CR-V 6150 / CR-V 50BV30 寫在每支產品上。Screwfix 88% 的 SKU 不標材質，標了就是差異化。
P0 英文包裝 + ISO/DIN 規格號放大 — 歐美買家看 DIN 3126E / DIN 3126C / JIS D76。寶暉已經有這些規格，但是藏在屬性欄。
P1 Multi-piece Set（25/50/100 件組合包） — Screwfix 組合包均價 £37-42，毛利率最高。寶暉有全頭型覆蓋，做組合包比任何品牌都容易。
P1 TX 全長度覆蓋（25mm→150mm） — 5 個長度同時推出。很多品牌只做 25mm 和 50mm，寶暉做到 150mm 是差異。
P1 Bit Holder 三款定位 — Magnetic / Quick Release / 100% Stainless，三種客群三種定價。
P2 進入 Masonry Drill — Screwfix 第二大品類（191 SKU），但需要新供應鏈。
P2 推 Impact-rated 產品線 — 用 CR-V 50BV30 材質，品牌標註 Impact Ready。
P2 DIN 3126E 位接頭系列強打 — 寶暉已有規格，但沒有作為賣點宣傳。
▸ BOTTOM LINE: 寶暉的優勢是「Security 規格覆蓋度」和「材質透明度」，弱勢是「無組合包」和「無 Masonry/Router 品類」。進入英國市場最佳切入點是 £5-15 的 Security Bits + Multi-piece Set，用材質鋼號差異化，不要跟 Erbauer 拼低價。
